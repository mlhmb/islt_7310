Usually, I love to give them a tailor-made video clip as a memorable gift while they promote to the next class. I have not had any formal web lesson training before; however, I have tried to create a simple one by using wix. It is a powerful tool to build a website for those people who don't have a strong code skill just like me. I don’t have to understand how it works behind the scenes as long as I upload some pictures and videos into the template that I choose.

Hopefully, this website will become their lifelong thing to do with their classmates. I can tell this will be one of the most valuable things they keep in their life.
